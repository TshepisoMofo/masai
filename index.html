FOUNDERS

Adv. Ishmael Semenya

Since 1996, Advocate Ishmael Semenya has been a National Executive Director of the Black Lawyers Association. He is also a committed member of the Bar Council and was appointed to the University of Witwatersrand Local faculty board. He has been instrumental in various litigations of commercial and constitutional cases, as well as being a contributing co-author of the South African... and Namibian Constitutions, respectively, having had his Senior Council status conferred in 1998. Advocate Semenya is also a chairperson of the appeals committee of the Micro Finance Regulatory Council and was also admitted as fellow of the International Academy of the Trial Lawyers. He has taken on duties as Acting Judge of the High Court and Acting Judge of the Labour Court. In 2001 he was the Assistant Commissioner of the Ellis Park Stampede, as well as the ice-Chairman for the Society of Advocates (WLD) for 2001/2002, and the chairman of the Johannesburg Bar Council from 2004 to 2006.

Sello Semenya

Sello Semenya graduated in 1986, making him one of the first black civil engineers in South Africa. Mr. Semenya has worked in the construction, research and consulting fields of civil engineering. He has been involved in the initiation and formulation of projects of varying size, in South Africa and Internationally. He ... is a founding director of the company and is extensively involved in Business Development as well as implementation management. Sello has been a member of various industry organisations and was part of a team that formulated the construction industry transformation charter. He has been involved in voluntary relief work, and was a member of a team that offered relief infrastructure rehabilitation during the South East Asia tsunami of 2004. He was based in the Maldives.

Kgabo Semenya

Kgabo Semenya is a Chief Executive Officer at EnviroXcellence Services with extensive project management skills in environmental impact assessments, environmental ... clean-ups at hazardous waste operation sites. He has been involved in environmental clean-ups at hydrocarbon contaminated sites, bioremediation of contaminated properties, and environmental management plans and waste management. He also holds memberships in various institutions, namely, Chief Executive Officer of Regodile Mining (PTY) LTD, environmental scientist at EnviroXcellence Services, environmental manager at Molosiwa Bruin and Associates, as well as National General Secretary of SABTACO.
